# GoldenEye Academy Course Catalogue

## Catalogue Rule

Every course must answer four questions quickly:

1. Who is this for?
2. What problem does it solve?
3. What will the learner practice?
4. What should the visitor do next?

Default CTA: Ask for Course Help.

## 1. IELTS Masterclass for Band 7+

Hook: Planning abroad but unsure how to reach your target band?

Best for: study abroad applicants, migration-focused students, scholarship applicants.

Core outcomes:

- Understand IELTS scoring.
- Improve listening, reading, writing, and speaking.
- Practice with weekly mock tests.
- Get feedback on weak areas.

Lead question: What destination, intake, and target band are you aiming for?

CTA: Get IELTS Roadmap.
