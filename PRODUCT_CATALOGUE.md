# GoldenEye Academy Product Catalogue

## Catalogue Purpose

This catalogue defines the sellable offers that should appear across the website, CMS, WhatsApp replies, and admin-managed content. Each product should push one clear action: get the visitor to leave details or send a quick message.

## Product Positioning

GoldenEye Academy sells clarity before classes. The core product is not only a course; it is a guided decision path that helps students, parents, abroad applicants, and job seekers avoid choosing the wrong program.

Primary CTA for every product: Ask for Course Help.

## Product 1: Free Course Roadmap Help

Hook: Not sure what to study next? Do not guess.

Audience: undecided students, parents, SEE/Plus Two graduates, job seekers, abroad applicants.

Promise: a short goal-mapping session that recommends the right course, test, skill, or next step.

What the visitor gets:

- Goal and current-level diagnosis.
- Course comparison.
- Timeline and budget discussion.
- Recommended next step.
- Follow-up by phone or WhatsApp.

Lead trigger: "I need help choosing the right program."

Best CTA copy:

- Get My Roadmap
- Ask for Course Help
- Help Me Choose

## Product 2: Study Abroad Test Prep Pathway

Hook: Planning abroad? Pick the right test before you pay for classes.

Audience: students targeting Australia, UK, Canada, Japan, Korea, or other international pathways.

Includes:

- IELTS Masterclass.
- PTE Elite Academic Training.
- Japanese JLPT N5/N4.
- Korean EPS-TOPIK.
- Help with test selection and timeline.

Trust angle:

- Test format guidance.
- Mock test practice.
- Score diagnosis.
- Destination-aligned recommendation.

Lead trigger: "Tell us your destination, intake target, and current English/language level."

## Product 3: Job-Ready Computer Skills Package

Hook: Need a job-ready skill fast? Start with the tools every office expects.

Audience: job seekers, admin/front desk applicants, students, professionals upgrading workplace skills.

Includes:

- Word, Excel, PowerPoint.

- File management.
- Office documentation and reporting.

Trust angle:

- Practical daily office tasks.
- Interview-relevant confidence.
- Suitable for beginners.

Lead trigger: "Tell us your current computer level and job goal."

## Lead Capture Rules

- Every product page must show a quick-help CTA above the fold.
- WhatsApp copy must mention free guidance, not only "message us."
- Forms should ask only for essential details: name, phone, email, course/goal, message.
- Do not promise guaranteed visas, jobs, scores, or outcomes unless backed by a real policy.
- Use urgency carefully: "Ask early: batches fill quickly" is acceptable; fake seat counts are not.
